In particular, we show that the asymptotic linear syzygies of Hirzebruch surfaces embedded by \(\mathcal{O}(d,2)\) conform to Ein, Erman, and Lazarsfeld's normality heuristic. We also show that the higher degree asymptotic syzygies are not asymptotically normally distributed.
