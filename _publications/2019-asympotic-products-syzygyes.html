--- layout: publication year: 2019 title: 'Asymptotic Syzygies for Products of Porjective Spaces' authors: - Juliette Bruce doi: arxiv: "1904.04944" status: - Published type: - Research journal: Journal of Algebra journal_tags: J. of Algebra journal_vol: 649 journal_year: 2024 journal_issue: no. 1 journal_pages: 347-391 journal_url: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0021869324001479?via%3Dihub tags: - Syzygies - Toric Geometry pdf: https://www.juliettebruce.xyz/papers/Asymptotic-syzygies-for products-of-projective-spaces-(JOA).pdf highlight: --- We study the asymptotic non-vanishing of syzygies for products of projective spaces. Generalizing the monomial methods of Ein, Erman, and Lazarsfeld we give an explicit range in which the graded Betti numbers of \(\mathbb{P}^{n_1}\times \mathbb{P}^{n_2}\) embedded by \(\mathcal{O}_{\mathbb{P}^{n_1}\times\mathbb{P}^{n_2}}(d_1,d_2)\) are non-zero.
